Monster Level

The monster's strength (level) is determined by the base level and correction set for each quest.
Levels are set separately for boss monsters and small monsters, but are basically the same.
In the case of quests with fixed levels, all small monsters will have the same individual values.

Map + day/night correction (favorite season)
　　　※In the case of consecutive hunts, the strength may increase with each kill. (The maximum rank increase is №16.)
