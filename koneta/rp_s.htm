SRP
  • Weapon multiplier increases by LV according to SR
  • Once you reach SR100, you can take the "storm style" test!
  • Secret books for other weapons cannot be acquired until SR 200 is reached
  • Once you reach SR300, you will be able to create hiden armor!
  • For hunter's quests, lower rank quests are multiplied by 0.6
  • SR401～：Obtained SRP will be corrected by the number of people
  • SR501～：SRP obtained will be corrected by SR
    　(This correction is excluded with premium course.)
  • The base value of SRP is twice the HRP.
  • If you go on a quest with a hunter under HR30, you will receive 1.5 times the SRP!
  • For hardcore quests, HRP is 1.5 times (for weapons of appropriate rarity: 3 times), and SRP is 4 times for weapons of appropriate rarity.
  • You can get about 1/3 (MAX3000SRP) of the SRP required for the next SR when you clear a quest by receiving a point bonus from daily benefits.
