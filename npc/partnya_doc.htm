Partnya

They will accompany you on quests and help you defeat monsters. [HR1~]
Unlike Halks, Partnya's are treated as a party member.
By raising them, you can increase their Health, attack power, etc.
You will be able to hire more partnyas when the previous one reaches rank 300.

Status
The initial and maximum values of their abilities are determined by the partnya's nature. They also increase by training.
Increasing the PNR (partnya rank) increases the value of the abilities.
Normal quest: HRP earned x 0.25
G rank quest: GRP earned x 1.25

Partnya Item Pouch
Extra pouch available only when accompanied by a partnya.
At first, it is 1 slot, but it increases for every PR100, and the max is 5 slots at PR400.
With assist course active, +5 slots will be added (max. 10 slots).

Quest Accompaniment
The following bonuses will occur if your partnya accompanies you on a quest. Numbers change depending on luck.
Hunters' Festival: Bonus of up to +15 souls
Great Voyage Festival: Up to 1.8x CP and score increase *outdated*
