Talisman Exchange

You can exchange various Talismans by talking to the Combiner.
Hunting Medals will be received from the guild master upon reaching milestones according to the number of specific monsters defeated.
Each talisman can be converted back to a Hunting Medal by spending 100 GCP.
The effect of the talisman is applied only in the order of [blue] < [red] < [yellow] < [white] < [brown], whichever is higher.
